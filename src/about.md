## My Career

**I bring a creative and inclusive approach to any project**:

- **My path.** After finishing college with a diploma in Art and Design, I spent several years in customer service roles, where I developed a strong understanding of people, communication, and problem-solving. I later moved into a Trainer and Learning Designer role and quickly discovered a passion for instructional design and learning content creation. After four years of managing my responsibilities between training delivery and learning design, I transitioned into a full-time learning design role as a Junior Courseware Developer.

- **My Skillset.** Creating accessible and inclusive learning for a worldwide audience; managing the project lifecycle from scoping to sign-off. Authoring inductions, internal processes, compliance and software training for regulated industries. Creating content for face-to-face, e-learning and blended learning experiences using adult learning theory. Experimenting with AI tools for efficiency, while also adding a human flair with illustration and graphic design.

- **My Toolkit.** Articulate Storyline, Rise, Camtasia, Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop and Illustrator), Microsoft Office, Procreate


## What I care about

I believe learning and development plays a vital role in the success of any organisation. Whether it’s helping employees grow and thrive in their careers or giving customers the knowledge and confidence to succeed with a product, great learning experiences can make a real difference.

I’m passionate about moving beyond training that simply ticks a box. Instead, I focus on creating learning that is engaging and genuinely useful. I focus on experiences that help people build confidence, develop skills, and achieve their goals.

## Beyond work

I love crafting, painting and hiking in nature. In the summer, you will probably find me in a field enjoying a music festival.
