## What I designed

I was tasked with creating a short microlearning course for vehicle recovery drivers to understand new government legislation on using red warning lights on their vans. 

Accessibility was key with this project, the employees involved in this training may have lower reading comprehension compared to the rest of the workforce, so I had to word the content carefully.

## Tools Used

Articulate Rise, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, Canva
