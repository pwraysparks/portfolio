## What I designed

This interactive guide was part of a larger blended course on how storms affect buildings. Previous learners were having difficulty understanding and identifying types of storm damage based on the beaufort scale which is a measure of windspeed. I was tasked to create a simple and easy to navigate guide for the course.

## Tools Used

Articulate Storyline, Microsoft Powerpoint
