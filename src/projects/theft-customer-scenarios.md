## What I designed

I was tasked to create a fun and engaging way to test the knowledge of customer service agents on their understanding of a home insurance policy and how they would handle theft claims on the phone based on different scenarios. 

I created this branching scenario which allows the learner to manage calls, with a helpful team leader guiding the way. I also created the scenarios intentionally to include another chance for the learner to think further and get the answer right if they chose an incorrect response. Have a go at seeing what the different options do!
